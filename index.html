Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I place an order on Zojo Nepal?
To place an order, select your desired item, choose your variant color, and add it to your cart. Click on your shopping cart, choose your payment method, click "Confirm Purchase", and sign in with your Google Account to safely fill out your shipping information.
What payment methods do you support?
We officially support Cash on Delivery (COD) for flexible physical transactions, alongside instant mobile digital wallet payouts via eSewa QR code routing and Khalti QR code transfer systems.
Do I need a Google Account to buy from your store?
Yes, authenticating via Google Secure Sign-In is required right before final submission. This keeps your shipment parameters valid, tracks your order safely, and ensures secure database profiles.
How long does shipping take across Nepal?
Deliveries are usually handled within 2 to 4 business days depending on your specific ward location and nearby landmarks. Our automated confirmations route your package coordinates smoothly.
